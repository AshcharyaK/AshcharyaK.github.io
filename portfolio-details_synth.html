Analogue Synthesizer

  • Category: Analogue Design
  • Project date: May-June 2021

Description

This aim of this Year 1 project was to create an Analogue Synthesiser with where the user is able to generate sound waves with various pitches (frequency), amplitudes, power and frequency composition. I was part of a group of three and in particular, my section of design was the VCA and the power output stages with a collective collaboration on integrative sections like functional testing.

The basic design for the synthesiser was formed by 5 main blocks to achieve the effects mentioned above: Voltage-Controlled Oscillators (VCO), Voltage-controlled Filtering (VCF), ADSR, LFO and Voltage Controlled Amplifiers.
